"The Kiwi Smoothie"

Kiwi Smoothie Recipe

Description

This 4-ingredient kiwi smoothie is sweetened with a frozen banana to eliminate the use of ice. It is also packed with protein, and the combination of spinach and soy milk will keep you feeling full through the morning.

Ingredients:

  1. 2 kiwi, peeled
  2. 1 banana, frozen, broken into chunks
  3. 1 cup spinach
  4. 1/2 cup soy milk

Steps:

  • Place kiwi, banana, spinach, and soy milk in the jar of a blender; cover and blend until smooth, about 1 minute.
  • All done! Enjoy your smoothie!
